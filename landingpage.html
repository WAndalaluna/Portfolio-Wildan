Who am I?

Hi! I'm Wildan Rahmantya Andalaluna, an undergraduate student of Informatics Engineering at Universitas Padjadjaran with a strong passion for technology, innovation, and social impact. I thrive on learning new things, especially in the fields of machine learning, software development, and digital entrepreneurship.

Last year I'm grateful enough to be part of the Bangkit Academy 2024 Machine Learning Cohort, a prestigious program led by Google and supported by top industry players like Tokopedia, Gojek, and Traveloka. One of my proudest projects here is building a hybrid recommendation system for a food-tracking app called Ping! - Kaki Lima Tracking App using both content-based and collaborative filtering approaches.

Previously, I was selected for ImpactSEA 2025, a changemaker initiative supported by the U.S. Department of State and Fulbright University Vietnam. I also actively contribute to student organizations such as HIMATIF FMIPA Unpad and AIESEC, where I've held multiple leadership roles that helped me hone my skills in communication, coordination, and event management.

With a mindset driven by curiosity and impact, I'm always on the lookout for opportunities to grow, collaborate, and make meaningful contributions whether through technology, community development, or creative problem-solving.
